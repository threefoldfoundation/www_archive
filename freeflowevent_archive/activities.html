HIERBAS WORKSHOP


“Hierbas ibicencas” is a traditional drink, and the most popular liquor in Ibiza and Formentera.
During this workshop, we will start collecting the local herbs that are going to be used to make the liquor, and learn how to differentiate them.  They will also do a guided tour around the farm, where we can feed the animals.
At the end of the visit, they will offer us fresh juice, wine and a tasting of their local products like cheese, “sobrasada”, and Ibizan “cocas”.
And we will take home a 1L bottle of the hierbas you have just prepared!
