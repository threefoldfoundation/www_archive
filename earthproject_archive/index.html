ROAD SO FAR

2015, April
KAKUMA PROJECT

Uniting teachers offering a free education to African refugees

We shipped our laptops to Kakuma refugee camp. We went over there and set up power supply and internet connection. Then we united a 325 teachers across 45 countries who started to teach refugees English, Math and Science, for free.

2017, October
TeachSDGs + CLIMATE ACTION

1M+ Students, 100 Countries
1 Mission

We united all teachers willing to focus on the UN SDG's in education and launched the #TeachSDGs community with 40,000 teachers on board. Our Climate Action Project was covered by BBC, CNN and National Geographic and endorsed by Jane Goodall.
